Education

Work Experience

This entailed a great deal of physical labor and fine attention to detail as it involved finetuning shutters and blinds and then installing them to be symmetrical with each window and the house as a whole. Learned relevant on-the-job skills along with respect for authority and an eye for detail.

Volunteer Experience

Served as a full-time volunteer missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Johannesburg, South Africa and the surrounding areas from 2020 to 2022. Developed a strong work ethic by working twelve hour days in order to meet specific goals. Supervised teams of up to ten missionaries to ensure their well-being and dedication to meeting team goals. Submitted weekly reports in a timely manner as required.

Athletic Experience

Recruited to play for the Men's Volleyball team at Brigham Young University, Cooper Jarman has been a setter on the team since he returned from his volunteer service in Africa in August 2022. As a Division 1 collegiate athlete, he has come to understand the valuable social skills that it takes to be a great teammate along with physical and mental discipline.
